How I Motivate Myself Towards Online Learning?

At first, I could not adapt with new style of learning which is Online Learning because it is virtual and I find it hard to communicate with my lecturers and friends. Since Online Learning will go through for this whole year, I need to change my perspective towards Online Learning in order for me to continue being one of Dean's List in UiTM Machang.

I decided to motiavte myself by finding best quotes on Google to boost up my spirit towards Online Learning. So there are a few quotes that I found on Google :-

1.eLearning doesn't just "happen"! It requires careful planning and implementation. - Anonymous

2.In order to create an engaging learning experience, the role of instructor is optional, but the role of learner is essential. - Bernard Bull

3.Online learning is not the next big thing, it is the now big thing. - Donna J. Abernathy

From the website 23 Inspirational e-Learning Quotes written by Christopher Pappas

These quotes make me realize how important our intention towards study. No matter how you study, if you are willing to success in study, you will overcome it. Different kind of style in study may affect us too, but the most important thing is we need to keep our SPIRIT TO STUDY in order for us to success.
